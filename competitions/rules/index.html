Competition Rules

Guidelines put in place to provide a fair and fun competition

Rules and etiquette for the MITRE Capture the Flag

Rules

  • Absolutely no flag sharing! (Why help your competitors?)
  • You may not do anything to diminish the competition experience for other players on the system; if you are worried that you might break something, ask an operator in Discord (with a private message) for clarification. We love creativity, just clear it with us first.
  • No automated tool usage (nmap, metasploit, nessus) is allowed against our infrastructure. If you feel a challenge requires the use of one of these tools, please ask an operator to verify.
  • Foul play will not be tolerated (first offense is a warning, second offense is a 10-50 point deduction and third offense is a disqualification)
  • No bullying. This includes harassment, intimidation and threats.
  • Operator decisions are final

Etiquette

  • Keep it clean in IRC and MPAA G-rated conversations, please
  • Remember that operators in IRC channel are running the game
  • Be a good sport throughout the competition

Flag Formats

We have 3 different possible flag formats you may find during our competition. They are as follows:
  • MCA-8 Hex Digits, for example MCA-FFFFFFFF
  • MCA-16 Hex Digits, for example MCA-FFFFFFFFFFFFFFFF
  • SHA1 Hash, for example 55572e792981f50145f75649f53fcd3c :)
